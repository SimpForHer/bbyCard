THE GLIB BARD FALLS IN LOVE

Allow me to apprise you, of the story I've been thinking,
of the girl I can watch, for days without blinking,
her eyes are like a dream I couldn't get out of,
oh, so mesmerizing, like the diamond on a crown's top,
it pains my heart, to even imagine how life would be,
how empty my arms shall feel, devoid of the warmth of she,
i love her eyes,, her hair, her face, her hands, her smile,
for even a glimpse of her, makes my day worthwhile,
holding hands and walking through the rain,
making out surreptitiously in the empty train,
her touch makes me forget things, her hugs make me remember,
i can never get enough of her, at the end of the day my heart feels like ember,
i love it when she looks at me, with a cheery smile on her face,
it makes me feel ecstatic, in so many simple ways,
surely, there are still so many ups and downs,
one moment we're philosophers, and the next we're clowns
she elevates my pigmy heart, with just her laugh,
everything that we do, others only dream of,
i think of her every second, she's all that comes to my mind,
i could never let go of her, only the reaper's scythe can undo this bind,
she is what i need, i have never been more certain of it,
for she has everything i could wish for, her beauty, her charms and her impish wit,
i wish to spend my whole life with her, to every last bit,
for she knows it too, like puzzle pieces we fit.

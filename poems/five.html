greed of the depraved

perhaps i loved her too much,
and my obsession
made her feel suffocated,
overwhelming, bounded and such,
i did not mean to,
i would never,
but i was just too into her,
my love, became a cadaver,
maybe i,
am not what she searched for,
just a needy, depraved beggar,
neither sagacious, happy nor,
i asked for too much,
for someone who couldn't even go down her crust,
i yearned for something undeserved,
my insecurities threatened her trust,
will she leave me?
will I be alone?
have i overdone it?
will she, too, be gone?
am I not to expect,
what i think i deserve,
attention, attention and respect,
to be treated equal on this curve,
to be told i am loved,
it is not what the devil tells me,
she finds me pleasant and loveable,
certainly does not resent me,
would she tell me what i wish to hear?
what we have will go on, the end is not near,
i do not wish to start anew,
conclusions are something i still fear,
i just wish she would treat me,
like i am truly her dear,
tell me that she loves me,
more than anything near,
hold my hands, watch my eyes,
wrap her arms around me, hear my cries,
or was i just a replacement?
something to fill the space,
for i feel her resentment,
growing every passing day,
i do not know,
i can not feel,
oh i wish i could tell her,
everything over a pleasant meal,
i do not understand,
what lies underneath her veil,
does she simply not miss me,
is what we have really real?
